ROAD RUNNER

DISCORD YOUTUBE BACK

Road Runner is my latest project, and my first to feature multiplayer mechanics. The game also contains elements of survival, FPS, inventory management and vehicular combat, all taking place on a large procedural island. Road Runner is still in its earliest stages of development, but builds and a place to playtest are available in the Discord.
