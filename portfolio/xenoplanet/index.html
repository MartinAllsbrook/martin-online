Introduction

Xenoplanet is a beautiful open-world adventure game. Players can choose to explore an infinite procedural world, or rifle with danger from hostile robots. Whether they choose to take back their world or simply enjoy the freedom to explore, Xenoplanet offers players endless possibilities.

My work on panspermia consisted of developing the script to procedurally generate Xenoplanet’s world at runtime. Designing and implementing enemy AI and combat to produce mechanics and interactions that aligned with our desired aesthetics. As well as working as the general scripter creating the inventory system, world interactions, item drops and more.
