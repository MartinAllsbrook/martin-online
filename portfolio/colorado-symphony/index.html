ABOUT THIS PROJECT

The inspiration for the design of the postermailer came from an amalgamation of the Mexico 68 Olympic graphics and Mondrian’s art. In addition I really wanted to use an abstraction of classical music to inspire some of the elements of the poster and thought that the repeating 5 line pattern common in sheet music would work very well. I used elements from the two styles mentioned in addition to elements of sheet music to create an almost mesmerizing pattern of repeating lines and shapes that propagate throughout the poster mailer. I wanted to keep the typography simple and geometric to align with the graphics surrounding it and chose two very simple sans serif typefaces.
