ABOUT THIS PROJECT

This project was the winning entry of a logo design competition sponsored by NASA's Artemis Student Challenges and the Colorado Space Grant Consortium. The goal was to create a new logo for the GLEE lunar mission that represented the project and included imagery of the earth, moon, and “LunaSats” being sent. The competition ran from January 7th, to January 28th 2022, and the graphics I designed are used as the mission's logo.
